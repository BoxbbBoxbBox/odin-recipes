Zebra Pudding

Description

This is an important pudding. It includes alternating layers of vanilla and chocolate pudding. You can use tapioca pudding instead of vanilla, but Mendel will think it's gross

Ingredients:

  • Chocolate pudding
  • Vanilla pudding

Steps

  1. Put chocolate pudding on plate.
  2. Add stripes of vanilla pudding on top of the chocolate pudding. Ensure there is room to push vanilla pudding out of the way if desired.
  3. Notify Mendel of presence of zebra pudding.
