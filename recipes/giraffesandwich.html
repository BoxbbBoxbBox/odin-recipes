Giraffe Sandwich

Description

This is an important sandwich. It includes elements that Mendel likes, but not in combination. But he will request it anyway.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate pudding
  • Peanut butter
  • Gluten-free bread - 1 slice

Steps

  1. Put bread on plate.
  2. Slather peanut butter on bread.
  3. Place a few splotches of chocolate pudding on top of peanut butter. Do not fold sandwich.
  4. Notify Mendel of presence of giraffe sandwich.
