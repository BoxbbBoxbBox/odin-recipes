Oyster Potato

Description

This is an important potato. By adding the chocolate element, you enhance Potat-ality by 74%.

Ingredients

  • Sweet potato
  • Butter
  • Cinnamon - lots
  • Lebby's chocolate balls - 2 to 4 balls

Steps

  1. Bake sweet potato in microwave for 1 potato, plus 30-60 seconds if necessary.
  2. Cut potato in half, then cut slits through center of each half of potato.
  3. Slather butter on potato.
  4. Generously sprinkle cinnamon on top.
  5. Extract chunks of potato as appropriate, to make them easier to grab.
  6. Place chocolate balls in conspicuous spots on potato, ensure that Mendel sees them so he won't choke on them.
  7. Notify Mendel of presence of oyster potato.
